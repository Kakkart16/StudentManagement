About

Welcome to the Student Management System!
Our website is dedicated to simplifying the management of student information, subjects, and marks for educational institutions. We understand the challenges faced by schools and colleges in handling and organizing vast amounts of student data. Our Student Management System provides a comprehensive solution to streamline these processes and improve efficiency. The Student Management System is a web-based application designed to streamline and automate the management of student information, subjects, and marks. It provides an efficient and organized platform for educational institutions to manage and track student data effectively.
In addition to student records, the system also maintains a comprehensive list of subjects offered by the institution. Each subject is associated with a subject ID and a name, allowing for easy identification and organization. Furthermore, the system enables the recording of student marks for each subject. The student_marks table links the student ID, subject ID, and marks, providing a relational structure to track and manage student performance.
The Student Management System incorporates functionalities for CRUD operations (Create, Read, Update, Delete) on student records and subjects.

Features

  • Manage student information
  • Track subjects and marks
  • View student performance
